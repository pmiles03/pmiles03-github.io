About

Small batches, no shortcuts.

We started Tŷ Coffi because Cardiff didn't have anywhere that roasted on-site and actually let you watch it happen. Every bag on our shelf was drum-roasted within the last two weeks, and every cup pulled behind the counter is from that same batch.

No syrups, no shortcuts — just well-sourced beans, treated properly, served by people who taste every new batch before it goes on the menu.
